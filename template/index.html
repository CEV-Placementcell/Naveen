ABOUT US


The Training and Placement Cell at College of Engineering Vadakara plays a vital role in shaping the careers of our students. Our mission is to provide our students with the necessary skills, training, and exposure to excel in the industry. We achieve this through a range of initiatives, including soft skills training, technical workshops, industry guest lectures, and internship programs. Our team works closely with industry partners to understand their requirements and tailor our training programs to meet their needs.

The placement cell has an impressive track record of placing our students in top companies across various sectors. We have established strong relationships with leading companies, including Infosys, TCS, UST global, etc to provide our students with a wide range of job opportunities. Our placement process is transparent, efficient, and student-friendly, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to take on the challenges of the industry. We are proud of our placement statistics and look forward to continuing our efforts to provide our students with the best possible career outcomes.
